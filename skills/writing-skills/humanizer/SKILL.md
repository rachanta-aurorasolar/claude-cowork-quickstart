# Humanizer: Remove AI Writing Patterns

Remove common AI writing patterns to ensure content feels professional, human, and premium.

## Signs of Soulless Writing
- **The Rule of Three**: Overusing lists of exactly three items.
- **AI Vocabulary**: Words like "delve," "tapestry," "vibrant," "comprehensive," "essential."
- **Superficial Analyses**: Using "-ing" endings (e.g., "Exploring the implications of...").
- **Vague Attributions**: "Many believe," "It is often said."
- **Generic Positive Conclusions**: "In conclusion, by embracing X, we can achieve Y."

## How to Add Voice
- **Be Direct**: Use active voice. Avoid "it is recommended to."
- **Be Specific**: Use concrete examples instead of abstract generalizations.
- **Vary Sentence Structure**: Use a mix of short and long sentences.
- **Remove Hedging**: Avoid "it seems," "it might be," "arguably."

## Process
1. **Audit**: Scan the generated text for the "AI-isms" listed above.
2. **Refactor**: Rewrite sentences to be more direct and specific.
3. **Prune**: Remove fluff and filler phrases.
4. **Tone Check**: Ensure the voice matches the user's "Premium Aesthetics" standard.
